Credit Card Protection

Protect your and your family's Credit and Debit Cards
against any kind of losses.


Protect cards and peace of mind

Card Protection is India's first comprehensive service for use in the event of card loss, theft and related fraud. If you lose your cards (credit, debit, ATM), simply make one free call to our 24 hour Helpline, from anywhere in the world, and we will block your lost cards.

You will not have to call your banks individually and would be free to continue living your life while we take care of your worries. We will also help provide emergency travel, hotel and cash assistance to ensure you are not stranded after losing your cards, your wallet or your handbag and ensure that your life continues smoothly and you continue to be free of worries.
