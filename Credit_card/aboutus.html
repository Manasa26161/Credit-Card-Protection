Some Solution to enhance credit card safety:

1.Keep Your Card Safe:
Always keep your card in a secure place, such as a wallet or purse. Do not leave your credit card lying around or let anyone else use it.

2.Memorize Your PIN:
Your PIN is the most critical security feature for your card. Make sure to memorize your PIN and never write it down or share it with anyone, including family and friends

3.Check Your Credit Card Statements Regularly:
Regularly checking your credit card statements can help you identify any unauthorized transactions

4.Keep Your Contact Information Up-to-Date:
Ensure that your bank or credit card company has your current phone number and email address so that they can contact you in case of any suspicious activity.
